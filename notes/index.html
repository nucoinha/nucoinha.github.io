Welcome to our website where we discuss various concepts related to finance and Nucoin.

Volume in Finance

Volume refers to the number of shares or contracts traded in a security or an entire market during a given period of time.

What is a Candle Bar?

A candlestick chart is a type of financial chart that shows the price movement of a security, derivative, or currency. Each "candlestick" typically represents one day's worth of price data.

Color Representations

In financial charts, colors are often used to represent different aspects such as price movement, volume, and trends. Green typically represents upward movement, while red represents downward movement.

Frozen and Melting in Nucoin

In the context of Nucoin, "frozen" refers to the process of locking Nucoins for a certain period, while "melting" refers to unlocking them after the specified period has passed.
